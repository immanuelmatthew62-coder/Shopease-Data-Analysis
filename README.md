📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data for ShopEase, a retail business operating across multiple regions in Nigeria. The goal was to turn raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights that can support better business decisions.

The workflow covers the full analytics pipeline — from data cleaning in SQL, to exploratory analysis, and finally an interactive Excel dashboard for visualization.

🧹 Data Preparation (SQL)

The raw dataset contained inconsistencies and formatting issues that could affect analysis accuracy. To resolve this, the data was cleaned and standardized using SQL.

Key steps included:

Fixing inconsistent product names and typos

Standardizing categories, regions, and city names

Converting date fields into proper analytical format

Removing data inconsistencies

Creating calculated fields such as total sales


This step ensured the dataset was reliable and ready for analysis.

📊 Expository Data Analysis & Findings

After cleaning the data, exploratory analysis was conducted to understand business performance and uncover key patterns.

📈 Sales Trends

Revenue performance varied across the year, with noticeable peaks and dips in different months. This indicates that sales are influenced by seasonal demand patterns and customer buying cycles.

🏆 Product Performance

A small number of products generated a large portion of total revenue. These top-performing products consistently outperformed others, showing strong customer preference and demand concentration.

At the same time, several products contributed minimally, suggesting opportunities for optimization in product strategy and inventory management.

🗺️ Regional Performance

Sales were not evenly distributed across regions. Certain regions contributed significantly more revenue than others, highlighting clear geographic differences in market strength.

This suggests that some regions represent stronger business opportunities and should be prioritized in sales and marketing strategies.

📦 Category Performance

The Electronics category was the dominant revenue driver, significantly outperforming Home Appliances.

This shows that customer demand is heavily skewed toward electronic products, making it the core category for revenue generation.

👥 Customer Behavior

Customer segmentation revealed that a small group of high-value customers contributed a large portion of total revenue.

This follows a typical Pareto pattern, where a minority of customers generate the majority of sales.

💳 Payment Trends

Customers showed a strong preference for digital payment methods such as card and bank transfers compared to cash payments. This reflects increasing adoption of cashless transactions.

📌 Key Insights Summary

Revenue fluctuates across months, showing clear seasonal patterns

A small number of products drive most of the sales

Regional performance is uneven, with specific regions dominating revenue

Electronics is the most profitable product category

High-value customers contribute disproportionately to revenue

Digital payments are the preferred transaction method
